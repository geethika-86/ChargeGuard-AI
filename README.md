# ChargeGuard-AI
AI-powered predictive maintenance for EV charging infrastructure using machine learning.
---

## Overview

ChargeGuard AI is an intelligent predictive maintenance system designed for EV charging stations.

Instead of waiting until chargers fail, the system forecasts future charging demand, detects abnormal behavior, computes charger health scores, prioritizes maintenance, and estimates business impact.

---

## Problem Statement

Most EV charging stations rely on reactive maintenance.

This causes

- Unexpected charger failures
- Revenue loss
- High maintenance cost
- Poor customer experience

ChargeGuard AI enables operators to detect degradation early and perform preventive maintenance.
